The Lore Of SSC

Long ago there was a village of darkness who's past was full of death. Now nothing but bones in graves filled the village. No life and no movement... This was until decades past and a force swept through the village a force so strong it struck life into the bones that were once lifeless. And before long the skeletons began to rise from their graves and roam the village once again. Dressing themselves in the cloths left from the past.  Alas the village came to be. The skeletons amongst each other began to experience their identity renewal. One by one, with new significance, they each began to roam. The Sneaky Skeletons went from nothing and lifeless to power and riches. As the skeletons became more aware of themselves they would begin to roam, sneaking around blending into different communities. The skeletons weren’t just strong, but sly and tricky. They would continue to cause chaos within the cities that they would visit. This club soon arose within no one’s future prediction. They became the Sneaky Skeleton Club, all unique and all powerful.
